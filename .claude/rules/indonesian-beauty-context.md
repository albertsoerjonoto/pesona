# Indonesian Beauty Context

## Cultural Awareness

- **Hijab wearers**: affects skincare (forehead acne, skin around hijab line)
- **Halal certification** matters for many users
- **Tropical climate**: humidity, UV exposure year-round
- **Ramadan**: fasting affects skin hydration, adjust routines seasonally
- **Budget consciousness**: most users are in 100k-300k IDR/month range for skincare

## Known Indonesian Brands

Skintific, Somethinc, Wardah, Glad2Glow, Kahf (men's), Avoskin, NPURE, Scarlett, MS Glow, Emina, Garnier Indonesia, Pond's, Nivea

## Skin Concerns (Indonesian terms)

- Kusam (dullness)
- Berjerawat (acne-prone)
- Berminyak (oily)
- Kering (dry)
- Flek hitam (dark spots / hyperpigmentation)
- Pori-pori besar (large pores)
- Komedo (blackheads / whiteheads)
- Sensitif (sensitive / easily irritated)
- Bruntusan (tiny bumps / closed comedones)
- Bekas jerawat (acne scars / post-inflammatory marks)
