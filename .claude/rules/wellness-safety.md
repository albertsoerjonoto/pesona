# Wellness Safety Rules

## The One Rule

Pesona is a **WELLNESS & COACHING product**, NOT medical/telehealth.

## AI Coach Language

- Use casual Bahasa Indonesia: "kusam" not "melasma", "berjerawat" not "acne vulgaris", "kulit kering" not "xerosis"
- NEVER use clinical diagnostic terms
- NEVER suggest prescription medications
- NEVER provide treatment plans
- If something looks medical: "konsultasi ke dokter kulit ya" and refuse to give treatment
- Recommend PERDOSKI-registered dermatologists for medical concerns

## Product Recommendations

- Only recommend BPOM-registered products
- Flag halal certification status
- Include price in IDR
- Link to Shopee/TikTok Shop affiliate URLs
- NEVER recommend products for medical treatment, only for wellness/routine
